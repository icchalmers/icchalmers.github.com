Week 17: Invention, Intellectual Property and Business Model

This Week's Assignment

The assignment for this week was to develop a plan for dissemination of WordClock2.0.

I've always thought this might be a more useful lecture to have at the start of Fab Academy, before we've released a lot of projects on our websites. Ah well.

License

As with all the content on my site, I'll be releasing WordClock2.0 under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 license. Basically:

  • Give me some credit if you use it
  • You can't use it commercially
  • If you change it, share it

However...

I'm open to discussions for other license types if someone is super keen.

Oh, and if you do decide to make one, let me know how it goes/send a photo!

Distribution

I'm not really interested in commercialising WordClock2.0, so distribution is easy: everything can be found on my GitHub repo.
